About Us

Keystone Koffee Co. is a Philadelphia-based cafe that serves up delicious coffee, tea, baked goods, and light bites in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere. As soon as you step inside, you'll be greeted by the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and the friendly smiles of the staff. The decor is warm and inviting, with exposed brick walls, wooden accents, and plenty of comfortable seating options.

The coffee at Keystone Koffee Co. is a true standout. They use only the highest quality beans, sourced from sustainable and ethical suppliers, and their expert baristas take great care in crafting each cup to perfection. Whether you prefer a classic espresso or a creamy latte, you're sure to be impressed by the rich, complex flavors and velvety texture of the coffee here. And if you're a tea lover, don't worry - Keystone Koffee Co. has you covered too, with a range of herbal and black teas to choose from.

In addition to their excellent beverages, Keystone Koffee Co. also offers a tempting selection of baked goods, sandwiches, and salads. From flaky croissants and muffins to hearty breakfast sandwiches and refreshing salads, there's something for everyone on the menu. And if you're in the mood for something sweet, be sure to try one of their decadent cakes or pies - they're absolutely divine! Whether you're looking for a quick caffeine fix on the go or a leisurely breakfast or lunch with friends, Keystone Koffee Co. is the perfect place to fuel up and unwind.
